About me

During my time at Lindbergh High School, I realized my interest in computer science and programming.

My confidence in myself has only grown since the hardships of moving at a young age unexpectedly, which has caused me to become independent when I need to be. On my free time, I enjoy playing and watching video games, discovering and creating new music, and spending time with friends. I listen to lots of music, and lately, I've had an interest in progressive metal and video game soundtracks (preferably retro).
